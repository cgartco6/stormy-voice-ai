# ⚡ Stormy Empire OS

### 🛑 CRITICAL RULES (Follow these or she breaks)
1. **The PWA Rule**: You MUST click the 3-dots in Chrome and select **"Add to Home Screen."** If you run her in a browser tab, Android will block her from opening Spotify or Settings.
2. **The First Tap**: Android blocks voice until you interact. **Tap the mic or type once** when you open her to activate her voice.
3. **The Asset Path**: Your photos MUST be in a folder named `assets`. 
    - `bg-1.jpg` (Normal)
    - `bg-2.jpg` (Furious)
4. **The "By" Command**: To search music, say "Play [Song Name] **by** [Artist]." She will now search Spotify instead of just opening the app.
